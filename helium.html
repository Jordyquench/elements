─── HELIUM ───

The first element we're going to be learning about is Helium (He), It is the first of the noble gases, the first place scientists discovered helium was on the sun!

Helium is quite similar to some other elements because it is found as a gas and has no smell or colour. It is a very popular element, this is becasue it is found everywhere, it makes up a small part of the air that you breathe.

USES

Have you ever gotten a balloon as a present? You know how that balloon always tries to float away? That's because there is helium inside.

DISCOVERY

Helium was first noticed on the sun, but was discovered on earth in 1895 by a scottish chemist called Sir William Ramsay

COOL FACT

If you get a balloon thats filled with helium and inhale it your voice will go really high and you will sound squeaky. It is dangerous to do though.
