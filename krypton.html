─── KRYPTON ───

This element may have the same name as supermans home planet but that is not where it is from, it's from right here on earth. It is a very small part of our atmospere here on earth, it only makes up one part per million of our atmosphere.

Unlike some of the other noble gases it has a colour, when it's a solid it is white but when it's a gas it has green and orange traits.

USES

Kyrpton was once used in finding out how long a meter is, over a while though the length of the meter changed to be a specific distance light travels in a vacuum.

DISCOVERY

Krypton was discovered right here on Earth by the scientists Ramsay and Travers in 1898.

COOL FACT

Like the mentioned before it has the same name as supermans home planet.
