─── NEON ───

Neon is a unique element and one of the most popular because it's a fun one. Most people have seen a big glowing sign at some point? this is neon, it looks like that when you electrocute it. without electricity, it isn't very cool.

Before people knew about the effects of electricity on neon it was a very boring element, it was once this was discovered that it became the element it is today.

USES

The most exciting uses for neon are in big light up signs, lasers and in cryogenics (freezing things very quickly).

DISCOVERY

Neon was discovered in 1898 by a pair of scientists called Ramsey and Travers.

COOL FACT

A cool fact about neon a lot of people wouldn't know is that stable forms of neon are produced within stars.
