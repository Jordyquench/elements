─── XENON ───

The name is derived from the Greek 'xenos' meaning stranger.

Xenon is a trace gas found in the Earth's atmosphere to the extent of about one part in 20 million, According to the Los Alamos National Laboratory. This makes it very rare. It is also found in Mars' atmosphere

USES

Xenon is used in camera flashes, in motion picture projection, and in high pressure arc lamps to produce ultraviolet light.

DISCOVERY

Xenon was discovered in 1898 by William Ramsay and Morris Travers. Earlier, Ramsay and Travers discovered the other noble gases krypton and neon.

COOL FACT

Jupiter has an exceptionally high amount of xenon, almost three times that of the Sun.
