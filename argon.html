─── ARGON ───

A lot of jokes are made about how long it took for scientists to discover argon because it is the most common noble gas, it makes up more than 1% of the atmosphere. at the time though it would have been impossible to discover it any earlier because the equipment just wasn't available.

For a long time scientists didn't think argon combined with any other elements but they were wrong, they realised they were wrong and now it is the element it is today.

USES

Some of the things argon is used in include light bulbs, welding and even in vacuum tubes.

DISCOVERY

Argon was discovered in 1984 by Lord Rayleigh and Sir William Ramsey.

COOL FACT

A cool fact about Argon is that it is used when scientists grow glowing silicon crystals because it doesn't react with the silicon.
